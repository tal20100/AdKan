# Competitors landscape — the rest of the category

Brainrot + Unrot get their own deep dive in `brainrot-unrot-learnings.md`. This file covers the next tier, sizes up the US/IL market, and states the whitespace argument AdKan exploits.

---

## Opal — the polished incumbent

- Pricing: $19.99/mo, $99.99/yr, **$399 lifetime**. The most expensive in category.
- Paywall: gamification + cute design behind paid tier.
- Standout: "gemstone" milestone leaderboard. Confirmed FamilyControls user.
- Social: a leaderboard community feature exists but isn't positioned as primary.
- Takeaway for AdKan: at ₪99 our lifetime tier is ~10x cheaper than Opal's. That's a marketing line.
- Source: https://www.refocusapp.co/articles/best-screen-time-apps-for-iphone

## Jomo — the closest competitor in spirit

- Pricing: $5.99/mo, $29.99/yr, $99.99 lifetime. Most affordable in the "polished" tier.
- Paywall: freemium — focus tools + friend competition unlock at paid tier.
- Standout: **"Squads" — friend leaderboards + built-in competition.** NFC tag integration for unlocking habits.
- Social: this is the one competitor that does what we do, BUT squads are a secondary feature buried behind focus tools. The app's primary loop is still self-tracking.
- Onboarding: behavior-focused (templates, journaling).
- Takeaway for AdKan: Jomo proved social works in this category. We make social the FIRST screen, not a tab buried behind focus.
- Source: https://www.refocusapp.co/articles/best-screen-time-apps-for-iphone

## One Sec — the research-backed minimalist

- Pricing: among the cheapest.
- Paywall: breathing-exercise intervention before app launch.
- Standout: positioned as "research-backed." No social, no avatar, no leaderboard.
- Takeaway for AdKan: the breathing-gate mechanic is legitimate — we could add it as a Premium "challenge mode" in v1.1 post-MVP.

## ScreenZen — the free option

- Pricing: almost entirely free.
- Paywall: minimal.
- Standout: high customization.
- Takeaway: our free tier needs to feel at least as capable as ScreenZen's, or we lose the price-sensitive segment entirely.

## Clearspace — mindfulness + friction

- Pricing: $3.99/mo, $12.99/yr, $29.99 lifetime. Cheapest lifetime in category.
- Standout: mindfulness-based (breathing, squats, pushups as friction gates), tracks app opens.
- Takeaway: our ₪99 lifetime is 3.3x Clearspace's. Price positioning: premium-but-not-extortionate.
- Source: https://www.getclearspace.com/blog/post/clearspace-opal

## Freedom — the power-user tool

- Cross-device, schedule-based, real blocking.
- Mature, expensive, targeted at adults with ADHD or serious focus needs.
- Not in our competitive set — different user, different price ceiling.

## Israeli App Store (2026) — the whitespace

No Israeli-native screen-time app found. Top productivity app on IL store is ChatGPT (US). Israeli developers have shipped time-management apps (**24me** — Apple "Best of Store" 2014; **Couch to 5K Runner** — health) but none in screen-time reduction.

**Translation: the Hebrew-first + social-first niche has no competitor.** This is the wedge AdKan exploits.
